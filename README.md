# 🚀 QuickMaid – On-Demand Maid Booking Platform

QuickMaid is a modern landing page for an **on-demand maid booking platform** focused on Tier 2 Indian cities.
It enables users to instantly book verified maids via WhatsApp and subscribe to monthly or annual home service plans.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

👉 https://yourusername.github.io/quickmaid-landing-page/

---

## 💡 Problem We Solve

Finding reliable domestic help in India is still **unorganized and inefficient**:

* ❌ No instant availability
* ❌ No verification / trust issues
* ❌ No replacement guarantee
* ❌ Dependency on local agents

👉 QuickMaid solves this with a **tech-enabled, trust-first approach**

---

## ⚡ Key Features

* 📱 **Instant Booking via WhatsApp**
* 👩 **Verified Maids (Aadhaar + Background Check)**
* 🔐 **OTP-based Job Start & End**
* 📦 **Subscription Plans (Monthly & Annual)**
* ⭐ **Rating & Review System**
* 💰 **Transparent Pricing (No Agent Commission)**
* 🔄 **Backup / Replacement Guarantee**

---

## 🧠 Business Model

* 💸 Pay-per-booking (Hourly services)
* 📦 Monthly subscription plans
* 📅 Annual plans (discounted pricing)
* 🏢 Future: B2B (Societies / Apartments)

---

## 🎯 Target Market

* Tier 2 & Tier 3 Indian cities (starting with Raipur)
* Working professionals & nuclear families
* Households looking for reliable domestic help
